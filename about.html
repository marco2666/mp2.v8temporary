Food Facts


Adobo

The word adobo is derived from the Spanish word adobar, which means “marinade” or “pickling sauce.”

Kare-Kare

Kare-kare is a Philippine stew (kare derives from "curry") that features a thick savory peanut sauce.


Sinigang

The reason why it is called “sinigang” is that it was a translation to the English word, “stew.”

Bulalo

Bulalo is native to the Southern Luzon region, particularly in the provinces of Batangas and Cavite.
